Artificer's Workshop

Seen above is the final presentation sheet for Artificer's Workshop, including three renders, an in-engine image, a texture map, and one of my reference images. In comparison to my initial greybox, it's come a long way.

My first greybox simply blocked out the structural elements of the room and the largest props in the scene, including the stairs, ceiling, workbench, desk, and counter.

Next I began to make some of the smaller props like the books.

With sufficient props modeled, I moved on to making materials and textures.

Last came lighting. I wanted to emulate candlelight, hence the warm tones. The light from outside is also slightly purple in reference to Artifice's story. Below are the final renders of the scene.
